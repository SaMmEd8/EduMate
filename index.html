About EduMate üöÄ

EduMate began as a project to build the ultimate smart study companion for college students. The goal was to create a single, intuitive platform combining everything a student needs: smart notebooks, AI-powered study assistance, a powerful scheduler, and tools to track productivity. This isn't just another app; it's designed to be your personal academic co-pilot, helping you study smarter, reduce stress, and achieve your goals. This project was built step-by-step, incorporating a full-stack architecture with a Node.js backend, a dynamic frontend, and integration with powerful third-party AI.
